questions

Good to know

Is BodyMind free?+

Yes. BodyMind runs on a free AI model, and there's no app-store fee — you install it straight from this page.

How does it estimate calories?+

Your meal — spoken, typed, or photographed — is sent to an AI nutrition model that returns a friendly summary with an estimated calorie and protein count. Estimates, not lab measurements, but great for everyday tracking.

Which devices can I install it on?+

Windows, macOS, Android, and iPhone/iPad. It installs as a real app with its own icon — no app store required.

Does the voice input need anything special?+

Voice logging uses your browser's speech recognition, which works best in Chrome or Edge. You can always type or upload a photo instead.

Is my data private?+

Your meals are stored in your own database, and the AI key lives on the server — it's never shipped to the browser.
